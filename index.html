NEW! Carbon Laser Facial

Carbon facials are taking the industry by storm & rightly so for its quick results with no downtime! A layer or carbon is applied to the skin which is then blasted away by the laser taking with it, dead skin cells, excess oil, dirt & debris. Combining this with laser genesis stimulates collagen to leave the skin looking and feeling rejuvenated. Treatment solution for all skin types.
